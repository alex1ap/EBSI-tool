EBSI - Business - Payment

Payment

  • EBSI
  • Business

This use case aims to provide a secure and efficient way for businesses to process cross-border payments. It enables secure processing of cross-border electronic payments, reducing the risk of fraud and errors in cross-border transactions and improving the speed and efficiency of cross-border transactions.

Examples

  • A Danish company needs to pay for services provided by a Swedish company for a project in Norway. With EBSI, the Danish company can securely and efficiently process electronic payments to the Swedish company, reducing the time and effort required for a physical visit to the bank and speeding up the payment process.
  • A Dutch company needs to pay for goods purchased from a Belgian company for a project in Germany. With EBSI, the Dutch company can securely and efficiently process electronic payments to the Belgian company, reducing the risk of fraud and errors in the payment process.
