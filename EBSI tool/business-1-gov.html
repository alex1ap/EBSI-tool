EBSI - Business - e-Government

e-Government

  • EBSI
  • Business

This use case aims to improve the efficiency and security of cross-border transactions between government agencies and departments. It enables secure information exchange and the delivery of public services across borders. This can benefit businesses by reducing the time and effort required to navigate complex cross-border government procedures, improving the speed and reliability of government services, and reducing the risk of fraud and errors in cross-border transactions.

Examples

  • A German company needs to obtain a license from a French government agency to sell its products in France. With EBSI, the German company can securely and efficiently apply for the license online, exchange information and documents with the French agency, and receive the license electronically, reducing the time and effort required for a physical visit to the agency and speeding up the licensing process.
  • A British company needs to obtain a permit from a Dutch government agency to construct a building in the Netherlands. With EBSI, the British company can securely and efficiently apply for the permit online, exchange information and documents with the Dutch agency, and receive the permit electronically, reducing the risk of errors and fraud in the permit application process.
